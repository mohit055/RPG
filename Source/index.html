Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson, formerly known as Max Power, is the father of the Simpson family. He is overweight, lazy, and ignorant, but also strongly devoted to his wife and children. He works as a low level safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant, although. He spends a great deal of his time at Moe's Tavern with his lifelong friends and a bartender. At home he can often be found sitting on the sofa mindlessly watching TV while snacking on food and drinking Duff beer. Homer has committed a wide range of crimes and infractions, from ticket-worthy offenses to felonies.

Marge Simpson

Marjorie Jacqueline "Marge" Simpson, is the happy homemaker and full-time mom of the Simpson family. With her husband Homer, she has three children: Bart, Lisa, and Maggie Simpson. Marge is the moralistic force in her family and often provides a grounding voice in the midst of her family's antics by trying to maintain order in the Simpson household. Aside from her duties at home, Marge flirted briefly with a number of careers ranging from police officer to anti-violence activist. She usually wears a long strapless green dress, red shoes, and a red pearl necklace.

Bart Simpson

Bartholomew JoJo "Bart" Simpson, A.K.A. El Barto and Bartman, is the mischievous, rebellious, misunderstood, and "potentially dangerous" eldest child of Homer and Marge Simpson, and the brother of Lisa and Maggie Simpson. Bart's a self-proclaimed underachiever constantly in detention. Bart's interests include Krusty the Clown, skateboarding, reading comic books, watching television, playing video games, helping Lisa solve various problems, and pulling off various pranks. His pranks are often elaborately complex and can lead to unfortunate consequences.

Lisa Simpson

Lisa Marie Simpson is a charismatic 8-year-old girl, who exceeds the standard achievement level of children her age. Not to everyone's surprise, she's also the moral center of her family. In her upbringing, Lisa lacks parental involvement of Homer and Marge, which leads to hobbies like playing saxophone and guitar, riding and caring for horses, and interest in advanced studies. In school, Lisa's popularity's affected by those who view her as a geeky overachiever, which leaves her with only a few friends. She inherited her father's chubby fingers, which somewhat affects her dexterity.

Maggie Simpson

Margaret Evelyn "Maggie" Simpson is the youngest child of Marge and Homer, and sister to Bart and Lisa. She is almost always seen sucking on her pacifier and, when she walks, she trips over her clothing and falls on her face. Maggie is the least seen in the Simpson family.Maggie has both good and bad qualities. Orally fixated,she is stubborn, acquisitive, and always seems to eventually get her own way. When she doesn't get what she wants. She looks exactly like her mother did as a baby. She was introduced in "Bart to the Future". She is very adorable. She was born on a Christmas Eve

Abrahim Simpson II

Abraham "Abe" Jedediah Simpson II, usually referred to as Grampa Simpson or simply Grampa, is the patriarch of the Simpson family, the father of Homer Simpson, Herbert Powell, and Abbey and the grandfather of Bart, Lisa, and Maggie Simpson. Abe is a World War II veteran later sent to the Springfield Retirement Castle by Homer. He is known for his long, rambling, and often inaccurate stories and general incompetence. Due to his apparent senility, Abe is often ignored by Homer and other members, resentful of this, or completely unaware of his being ignored.
